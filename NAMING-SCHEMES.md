Five options, each mapped to every agent. York stays York in all schemes.

James asked for this — pick one tomorrow, or mix and match.

## York's Take

**Streets is the one.** It's the most James — personal, tied to real places he walks past, and the meaning deepens over time. "Cedar caught a pattern" hits different from "Analyst caught a pattern" when you know Cedar is the road that connects everything in Cleveland Heights.

Positions and Single-Syllable are the safe picks. Clear, functional, no explanation needed.

Mythology risks being pretentious. Workshop risks being too cute (Broom, really?).

But this is James's call. Could also mix: Streets for the core team, descriptive for brainstorm agents that might never get built.
